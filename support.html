SwiftMove Support

We're here to help! Whether you have a question about your shipment, need assistance with our services, or just want to provide feedback, our team is ready to assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, please check our FAQs. You might find the answer you're looking for there!

How do I track my shipment?

You can easily track your shipment using the tracking number provided at the time of booking. Just enter it into the "Track Your Shipment" section on our homepage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards, mobile money (e.g., TNM Mpamba, Airtel Money), and bank transfers.

How do I get a quote for my shipment?

You can get an instant quote by visiting our "Services" page and using our online quote calculator. You'll need to provide details about your shipment, including origin, destination, and package dimensions.

What are your operating hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM CAT, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM CAT.

What should I do if my package is delayed or damaged?

Please contact our support team immediately using one of the methods below. Provide your tracking number and a detailed description of the issue.
